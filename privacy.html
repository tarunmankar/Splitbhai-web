Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

SplitBhai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile application, SplitBhai.

Offline-First Commitment: SplitBhai is designed to work locally. Your data stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

SplitBhai is an offline-first application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data, financial information, or expense records to any external servers. All data you enter into the app is stored locally on your own device.

2. Permissions

The app may require certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage/Files: To save your group and expense data locally on your device.
  • Internet: Only used for internal Expo updates and sharing features (like WhatsApp share), but no user data is sent to us.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share any information with third parties. If you use the "Share" feature, the data is handled by the specific app you choose (e.g., WhatsApp).

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from them.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
